About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Eaton's Burger Haven was born out of a passion for great food and the joy it brings to people. Our founder, Briana-Jo Eaton, a proud Jamaican, was inspired by her love for food and her desire to create a space where everyone could come together to enjoy a high-quality, mouth-watering meal. At Eaton's Burger Haven, every meal is crafted with care and passion.

As a local Jamaican business, we take pride in serving delicious burgers that bring people together. Whether it's a quick bite with friends, a family outing, or a solo treat, our ethos centers on quality, consistency, and comfort.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with an exceptional burger experience, offering great taste, excellent service, and a friendly atmosphere— while continuing to grow and expand throughout the Caribbean.

Our Vision

To become a beloved Caribbean burger brand, known for its high standards, flavorful menu, and commitment to community. We aspire to open branches across the region, sharing the love for great food with new customers in every corner of the Caribbean.
